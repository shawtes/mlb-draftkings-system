💡 KEY CONCEPTS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

GPP TOURNAMENT STRATEGY:
• Use CEILING projections (upside)
• Apply CONTRARIAN boosts (low ownership = boost)
• Low exposure limits (20% max per player)
• Aggressive stacking (QB+2WR, game stacks)
• Target: Top 1-10% finish

CASH GAME STRATEGY:
• Use FLOOR projections (safety)
• NO contrarian (play the chalk)
• High exposure limits (40% max per player)
• Conservative stacking (QB+WR)
• Target: Top 50% finish

CONTRARIAN OWNERSHIP TIERS:
• Leverage (<5%): +25% boost - Extreme contrarian
• Low (5-8%): +15% boost - Good contrarian play
• Medium (8-15%): No change
• High (15-25%): No change
• Chalk (>25%): -15% penalty - Fade in GPP

GAME ENVIRONMENT BOOSTS:
• High total (>48): +15% to all offense
• Big favorite (<-7): +12% RB, -5% QB
• Big underdog (>+7): +15% QB, +10% WR
• Bad weather: -20% QB/WR, +15% RB

STACKING OPTIONS:
1. Primary (QB+2WR) - 0.85 correlation
2. Tight (QB+WR+TE) - 0.80 correlation  
3. Game (QB+WR+OppWR) - 0.65 correlation
4. Bring Back (QB+2WR+OppRB) - 0.55 correlation
5. RB (RB+OppDST) - -0.40 anti-correlation
